Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2024

Overview

This privacy policy applies to Peak Timer ("App"), developed by Joaquin Arino ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

Subscription Information

For premium features, we use RevenueCat as our payment processor. RevenueCat collects:

  • App User ID (for subscription management)
  • Transaction information (transaction ID, subscription status)
  • Basic device information required for subscription management

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device and is not collected by us:

  • Workout configurations and timers
  • App preferences (colors, styles, and other customization settings)
  • Premium status

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Process and verify your subscription status
  • Provide and maintain premium features
  • Improve the app functionality and user experience

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for payment processing and subscription management. Their privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review RevenueCat's privacy policy for more information about how they handle your data.

We do not share your information with any other third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All payment processing is handled securely by RevenueCat
  • Your workout data and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All app data is stored locally on the device, and any subscription management is intended to be handled by parents or legal guardians.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
